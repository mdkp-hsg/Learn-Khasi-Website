iOS and WebApp Version

Do you want us to build an iOS and WebApp version for our Learn Khasi app?

Well it takes time, effort and skills to build them and currently it's in our plan but we need your support to help raise funds in order to build them.
You can help by making a donation below and select: "RAISE FUND FOR BUILDING Web and iOS App".
If you want to know our estimate budget and what is the cost to develop and deploy our app contact us via email: sngurshillong@gmail.com.

Why donate?

When you donate you help make our service available for free for everyone.
When you become a sponsor you will unlock more features than a normal user account.

Features List

Sl.NoFeaturePartially SponsoredFully Sponsored
1. Offline Dictionary One-Time Donors and Sponsors will be able to download the dictionary offline All Users will be able to download the dictionary offline.
2. Online Search One-Time Donors and Sponsors are not limited to the number of online searches they do per day. All users will not be limited to the number of online searches they do per day.
3. Level Up One-Time Donors and Sponsors can upgrade their levels up to level 3. All users can upgrade their levels.
4. LIVE Statistics The Number of users, words and sentences seen on the Home screen will be updated manually. The Number of users, words and sentences will be updated in real time automatically.
5. Notifications Users will get PUSH Notification if there is an app update or news. Users will be notified if their word or sentence has been translated. They can also turn off notifications.
6. Server Usage If there are 200 active users at the same time or our bandwidth reaches 10GB usage for the month. Online Features will not work for that time/month, only offline features will work. There will be no limit, all features will work as long as the expenses has not exceeded the donation amount received or the estimated budget.
7. Record Audio Clip of word or sentence Editors, One-Time Donors and Sponsors can record and upload audio clips. All users who reach level 2 can record and upload audio clips.
8. Report word or sentence as being wrong Editors, One-Time Donors and Sponsors can report an error in the word/sentence. All users who reach level 2 can report an error in the word/sentence.

How to donate?

Making a donation is only a simple 4(four) steps process.
Steps:

  1. Press "Donate Now" Button
  2. Enter your amount or select a preset, then press "Next"
  3. Enter your details and press "Proceed To Pay"
  4. Choose your payment method and press "Pay"
